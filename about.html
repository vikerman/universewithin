... a game about our recursive universes.

we are small objects in the universe. there are many objects that will come our way.
our goal is to avoid these objects to continue our cycle.

use left/right arrow keys, or tilt your device
'p' key to pause ; 'm' key to mute
there's 1 other key that does something secret.

originally created in under 48 hours for global gam jam 2012
infinite cycle of creation & learning; hand-drawn, with audio recorded on-site;
the embodiment of the ouroboros: "The Ouroboros represents the
perpetual cyclic renewal of life and infinity, the concept of
eternity and the eternal return, and represents the cycle of life,
death and rebirth, leading to immortality, as in the phoenix."
